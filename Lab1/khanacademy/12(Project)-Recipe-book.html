Recipe #1

  • Time: 1 million hour
  • Serves: For gymbro, food after training(I don't understand what they want from me here)
Ingredients Quantity
Engine oil 300 L
Antifreeze 700 L
Lithium battery (Iphone 15 Pro) 2
Uranus 1 kg
Moon rock 50 kg

Steps:

  1. Heat engine oil to 80 degrees Celsius, add antifreeze and mix very well for 600 thousand hours
  2. Place all the uranium in a moon rock and hit it on the floor for 200 thousand hours
  3. Combine both resulting components, place in an oven at a temperature of 300 degrees Celsius for 200 thousand hours
  4. That's it, your 31.8 billion calorie dinner is ready. Om-Nom-nom!
